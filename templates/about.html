About the PrepServer

Browser-guided docking package preparation for research teams.

AutoDock-Vina PrepServer is a public-facing Flask application for assembling reproducible AutoDock Vina job packages from receptor structures, docking-center definitions, and ligand inputs.

Overview

The app provides a structured browser workflow for receptor intake, docking box selection, receptor preparation, ligand intake, and downloadable package generation.

Why this tool exists

Docking preparation often becomes a fragile mix of manual file naming, coordinate tracking, and ad hoc folder assembly. PrepServer makes that setup more repeatable.

Who it helps

It is intended for researchers, students, and shared lab users who need a guided entry point for preparing docking jobs while preserving transparent downstream files.

What it does

PrepServer collects inputs, records docking centers, prepares receptor outputs, accepts common ligand formats, and builds portable or configured scheduler-oriented ZIP packages.

What it does not do

It does not guarantee docking accuracy, replace scientific review, automate medicinal chemistry decisions, or claim a closed-loop AI discovery workflow.

Companion tools

The site links to related tools for warhead review, degrader design continuation, E3 recruiter exploration, viral ligand triage, and molecular modeling support.
